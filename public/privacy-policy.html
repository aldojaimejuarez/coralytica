1. Introduction

Coralytica ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Reef Analytics Agent service.

This policy complies with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and is designed to meet the requirements for Meta's WhatsApp Business API applications.

Note for EU Visitors: While this policy is primarily designed for Australian law, we also respect the rights of EU residents under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). EU residents have additional rights including data portability and the right to be forgotten. Contact us for GDPR-specific requests.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Reef Analytics Agent service:

3.1 Personal Information

  • Phone Numbers: Your WhatsApp phone number when you interact with our Reef Analytics Agent
  • Voice Messages: Audio recordings of your voice queries when you use voice-to-text features
  • Text Messages: Written messages and business questions you send to our service
  • Contact Information: Any additional contact details you provide during our interactions

3.2 Business Data

  • Database Connections: Access credentials and connection details for your business databases (PostgreSQL, Google Sheets, etc.)
  • Query Data: Business questions and analytics requests you submit
  • Response Data: Generated charts, reports, and analytical responses

3.3 Technical Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service, including frequency and types of queries
  • System Logs: Technical logs for service optimization and troubleshooting
  • Device Information: Basic device and browser information for service compatibility

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide our Reef Analytics Agent service and generate responses to your business queries
  • Data Processing: To connect to your databases and extract relevant business data for analysis
  • AI Processing: To process your voice and text queries using OpenAI APIs (GPT and Whisper) for natural language understanding
  • Chart Generation: To create visual responses and charts using our local Python-based microservice
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our service functionality and user experience
  • Technical Support: To provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only used to provide our Reef Analytics Agent service and generate responses to your business-related queries.

5. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

5.1 OpenAI APIs

We use OpenAI's APIs (GPT and Whisper) to process your voice and text queries. When you send messages to our service:

  • Your queries are sent to OpenAI for natural language processing
  • OpenAI processes your data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not store your queries on OpenAI's servers beyond the processing period
  • OpenAI may use your data to improve their services (as per their terms)

5.2 Local Processing

Charts and visual responses are generated locally using our private Python-based microservice:

  • Chart generation happens on our secure VPS infrastructure
  • No third-party charting services are used
  • Your business data remains within our controlled environment

5.3 n8n Orchestration

We use n8n as an orchestration tool to manage automated workflows:

  • n8n helps coordinate data extraction from your databases
  • Workflow data is processed according to n8n's privacy policy
  • We maintain control over all workflow configurations and data flows

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Data Storage

  • Your data is stored on secure servers located in Australia
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Database connections are secured using encrypted protocols
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only

6.2 Security Measures

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Regular data backups with encryption
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

6.3 Data Retention

  • We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services
  • Query history is typically retained for 12 months for service improvement
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Data is securely deleted when no longer needed
